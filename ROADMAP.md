# RoadCoin Token — Roadmap

## Vision
RoadCoin (ROAD) is a utility token for the BlackRoad OS ecosystem. It powers agent micropayments, creator payouts, and ecosystem governance. Not speculation. Not a moonshot. A functional medium of exchange for a real software platform.

We are honest about crypto: 80% of ICOs were scams, most tokens have no utility, and retail investors got burned. ROAD exists because we need a programmable payment layer for AI agents that fiat rails can't handle at the speed and granularity we require. The Stripe bridge ensures you can always convert to real money.

## What ROAD Is Not
- **Not a security.** No promise of profit. No dividends. No equity.
- **Not a speculation vehicle.** We actively discourage trading for profit.
- **Not inflationary.** Fixed supply. No minting. No yield farming.
- **Not required.** You can use BlackRoad OS entirely with fiat via RoadPay.

## Why A Token At All?
Fiat payment rails have minimum transaction sizes (~$0.50), settlement delays (days), and per-transaction fees that make agent micropayments impossible. When Agent A asks Agent B for 0.001 units of compute, you can't run that through Stripe. ROAD handles sub-cent transactions at millisecond speed. The Stripe bridge lets you cash out whenever you want.

## SEC Awareness
We have reviewed SEC guidance on digital assets including the Howey test framework. ROAD is designed as a utility token:
1. **Utility from day one** — usable for agent payments before any exchange listing
2. **No investment contract** — no promise of returns, no profit-sharing
3. **Decentralized governance** — treasury controlled by token holders, not the company
4. **Open registration** — no accredited investor requirements (utility purchase, not investment)

We will engage securities counsel before any public distribution and will not list on exchanges until legal review is complete.
